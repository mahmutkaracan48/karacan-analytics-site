Unsubscribe / opt-out

We want our outreach to reach only people who find it useful. If you do not, we are sorry for the noise — here is how to stop it.

Fastest: reply STOP

Reply STOP, unsubscribe, or any short opt-out message to the latest email you received from us. Our system routes opt-outs to a permanent blocklist that prevents future outreach to your address and (where possible) your company domain.

Or email us directly

Email contact@karacan-analytics.com with the subject Unsubscribe. Include the email address you want removed.

What happens after you opt out

  • Your email is added to our permanent suppression list.
  • Future cold outreach to that address is blocked at our sending platform.
  • We do not maintain a marketing newsletter, so there is no recurring list to leave.
  • If you also want your company domain blocked, mention it in the reply.

Wrong contact?

If you are receiving outreach intended for someone else at your company, reply with the correct contact (or wrong contact) and we will route manually.
